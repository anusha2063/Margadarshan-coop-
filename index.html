About Us

Marga Darshan Mahila Sahakari Sanstha is a non-profit, women-led cooperative established in 2066 B.S. by a group of determined and visionary women. Born out of a shared dream to uplift and empower the local community, the cooperative has grown into a symbol of unity, strength, and resilience.

Since its inception, it has remained deeply rooted in the values of self-reliance, mutual support, and social responsibility. The organization primarily focuses on enhancing financial awareness among rural families and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.
