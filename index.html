About Us

Marga Darshan Mahila Sahakari Sanstha is a non-profit, women-led cooperative established in 2066 B.S. by a group of determined and visionary women. Born out of a shared dream to uplift and empower the local community, the cooperative has grown into a symbol of unity, strength, and resilience. Since its inception, it has remained deeply rooted in the values of self-reliance, mutual support, and social responsibility. The organization primarily focuses on enhancing financial awareness among rural families and promoting sustainable agricultural practices. Over the years, it has played a vital role in encouraging women's participation in leadership and decision-making roles, creating a platform where voices that were once unheard are now leading change. With a strong emphasis on inclusion and collective progress, Marga Darshan Mahila Sahakari continues to inspire and serve as a catalyst for grassroots transformation.
